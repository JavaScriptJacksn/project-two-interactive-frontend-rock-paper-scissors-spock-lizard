Rules

The rules of this game is simple, each move you can chose defeats two other moves. If the move you pick defeats the opponent's, you win! ...and of course, vice versa. List of rules:

  • Scissors cuts paper.
  • Paper covers rock.
  • Rock crushes lizard.
  • Lizard poisons Spock.
  • Spock smashes scissors.
  • Scissors decapitate lizards.
  • Lizard eats paper.
  • Paper disproves Spock.
  • Spock vaporizes rock.
  • Rock crushes scissors.

Player vs player game mode:

This game works a little on the trust of the users, so, if you are playing Player versus Player, in the name of sportsmanship, make sure to look away from the screen when the other player is chosing their move, thats cheating!
